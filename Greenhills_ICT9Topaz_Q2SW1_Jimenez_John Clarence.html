Shih Tzu Shih Tzus or 'Lion Dogs' have been around for 1,000 years or so.
Shih Tzus come in a weight of 9 to 16 pounds, and are between 8 to 11 inches standing.
They have long, double coats that can have many different colors.
Siberian Husky Thickly-coated, sled dogs of medium size, with great endurance and the ability to work in packs,
being able to pull light loads over frozen lands are what Siberian Huskys are known for.
Siberian Huskys are 35 to 60 pounds and stand at 20 to 23.5 inches tall.
They are born pack dogs and enjoy the company of other dogs
American
Foxhound		 American Foxhounds are good-natured, low-maintenance hounds.
They are closely tied to Revolutionary heroes and the estates of old Virginia.
They weigh in at 60 to 70 pounds and stand at the height of 21 to 25 inches.
